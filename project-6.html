Stella

For this project I created for tour and artist branding including a brand guide, deluxe album design, and merchandise for the fictional artist, Stella for her debut album "Shedding".

In this album, Stella shares her journey of self-discovery, self-expression, and identity as she navigates adolescence. While her stories are uniquely her own, she aims to connect with her audience, offering relatability and comfort.
